About me

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at KAIST, advised by Professor Joon Son Chung. I earned my M.S. supervised by Professor Joon Son Chung at KAIST. My research focuses on enhancing conversational and auditory experiences in challenging acoustic environments. I am also interested in exploring generative models and multimodal contrastive learning.
